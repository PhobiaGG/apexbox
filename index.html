The Journey

From a handheld prototype to race-grade telemetry. A timeline of ApexBox.

Jan 2025

Conceptual Inception

First prototypes developed as a handheld hiking GPS unit. Core GNSS signal processing and map rendering algorithms established.

June 2025

Automotive Pivot

Identified the need for high-speed telemetry in grassroots racing. Transitioned to the ESP32-S3 architecture and integrated the first OBD2 CAN bus bridge.

Oct 2025

The Ecosystem

Launched the ApexBox Companion App (Beta). Established secure BLE 5.0 streaming and implemented the VirtualDyno horsepower calculation algorithms.

Jan 2026

Hardware Finalization

Validated the MJF Nylon PA12 heat-resistant housing. Stabilized 25Hz multi-constellation GNSS performance for pinpoint accuracy.

Feb 2026

Patent Pending

Official filing of proprietary sensor fusion and data bridge patents. Launching the Founders Edition waitlist and community Discord.

Q2 2026

Production Launch

Full-scale production run and global shipping. Expansion of the ApexBox Analysis Suite with web-based telemetry visualization.
