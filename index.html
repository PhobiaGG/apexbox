Built for the Trail

Professional-grade navigation in a pocket-sized, rugged device.

🌏

Offline Maps

Pre-loaded OpenStreetMap tiles on SD card. No internet or cell service needed -- ever.

🛰

Multi-GNSS

GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou. 2.5m accuracy with fast satellite lock in under 30 seconds.

🧭

Sensor Fusion

9-axis IMU with hardware-accelerated fusion. Dead reckoning keeps you on track when GPS drops out.

🌡

Environmental Sensors

Barometric altimeter, temperature, humidity. Monitor weather trends and accurate elevation data.

🔋

Physical Controls

Rotary encoder and tactile buttons. Designed to work with gloves in any weather condition.

🔌

USB-C & Long Battery

8-12 hours of active navigation. USB-C charging. Power save mode extends life even further.

📈

Track Recording

Record your adventures as GPX tracks. Export, share, and review your routes on any mapping software.

🔈

Audio Navigation

Voice prompts for turn-by-turn guidance. Keep your eyes on the trail, not the screen.

Technical Specifications

What's inside the box.

Processor

  • ESP32 Dual-Core @ 240MHz
  • 520KB SRAM, 4MB Flash
  • WiFi + Bluetooth 4.2

Display

  • 3.2" IPS LCD (240x320)
  • 65K colors, dimmable backlight
  • Sunlight readable

GPS

  • u-blox SAM-M10Q
  • GPS / GLONASS / Galileo / BeiDou
  • 10Hz update, 2.5m accuracy

Sensors

  • BNO055 9-Axis IMU
  • BME280 Baro/Temp/Humidity
  • VEML7700 Ambient Light

Power

  • 3.7V LiPo (2000-3000mAh)
  • 8-12 hour runtime
  • USB-C charging

Physical

  • ~60mm x 90mm x 20mm
  • ~150g with battery
  • Splash-resistant enclosure
